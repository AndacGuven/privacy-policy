Privacy Policy

Ingredient Checker Scan Label

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit personal information such as your name, email, phone number, address, contacts, location, or photos. We do not use analytics tools that identify users.

2. Scanned Product Data

When you scan a product label, the App processes the image locally or via backend AI models to analyze ingredients. These images are not stored, not sold, and not linked to any user. Data is processed temporarily only to generate an AI result.

3. In-App Purchases (RevenueCat)

The App uses RevenueCat to manage subscriptions.

RevenueCat may collect non-identifiable information such as:

  • Anonymous App User ID
  • Purchase history (required for subscription validation)
  • Device type (iPhone, iPad)

RevenueCat does not receive personal identifying information. Their policy can be viewed here: RevenueCat Privacy Policy

4. Third-Party Services

The App may communicate with:

  • Apple App Store (subscription handling)
  • RevenueCat (entitlement verification)

No ads, analytics trackers, or profiling systems are used.

5. Data Security

No permanent user data is stored on servers. Temporary data is handled securely and discarded immediately after processing.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
